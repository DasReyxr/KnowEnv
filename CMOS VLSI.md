## Level of integration of chips

**Small Scale Integration (SSI)** - 10 to 100 gates per chip
**Medium Scale Integration (MSI)** - 100 to 1k gates per chip
**Large Scale Integration (LSI)** - 1k to 10k gates per chip
**Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI)** - more than 10k gates per chip


# Basics of transistor
A junction between p-type and n-type semiconductors is called a p-n junction. The p-n junction is the basic building block of many semiconductor devices, including diodes, transistors, and solar cells.

a Metal Oxide Semiconductor Field Effect Transistor (MOSFET) is a type of transistor that is widely used in digital and analog circuits. It consists of three terminals: the source, the drain, and the gate. The gate terminal controls the flow of current between the source and drain terminals by creating an electric field that modulates the conductivity of a channel between them.
